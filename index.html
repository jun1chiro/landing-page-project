    Learn to play the ancient board game enjoyed by millions of people worldwide!

    “Go” comes from the Japanese 囲碁 (Igo), and is also known as 圍棋 (wéiqí) in China and 바둑 (baduk) in Korea.

    Go is a fantastic game for your brain! It improves your concentration and memory while also making you an excellent problem solver.

    It's also excellent for controlling your emotions and maintaining mental flexibility and a keen intellect.

    Capture stones, control territories, read moves! It's all in the game of Go!
